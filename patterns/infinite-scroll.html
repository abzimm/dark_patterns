infinite scroll

AS THE user scrolls down the page, more content gets continuously added. This provides a seamless browsing experience, and works in tandem with mechanisms like pull-to-refresh.

Infinite scroll can be seen on popular apps like TikTok and Instagram. It's very easy to get lost in a flow state and become immersed in apps with infinite scroll. The simple, addicting nature of this pattern has made it commonplace across digital spaces.
