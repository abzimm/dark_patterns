guilty pleasure

USER DATA is often used to make their experience “better”, or more curated for their personal tastes. This can be seen in targeted advertising or algorithms that show people more of what they want.

While most apps use personalization to increase engagement, some also use it to sell things. For instance, Amazon recommends similar items every time something is viewed or interacted with. This encourages people to buy more than what they originally intended.
