recapture notif

RECAPTURE NOTIFICATIONS are sent out sporadically, designed to make users re-engage with an app. They can include updates, news, or promotions, and often have some sense of urgency.

Sometimes, the content is fabricated, such as flavor text telling users to “see what someone’s up to”, or to “keep up their streak”. Apps like Duolingo are particularly good at using recapture notifications.
